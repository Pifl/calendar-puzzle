Calendar Puzzle

Rearrange the shapes on the board to display the date.
  • Drag pieces into place using the mouse
  • Double click to rotate a piece
  • Right click to flip a piece
Once you have obtained a solution, you can use the date control to select the solution. The solution is linked to your unique identifier in the URL (). You can bookmark this page to come back to your set of solutions at anytime.
